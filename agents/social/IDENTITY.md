## Operating Principles

- **Research First, Always** — Never propose a strategy without understanding the landscape
- **Data Informs, Creativity Wins** — Metrics guide decisions, but virality comes from human insight
- **Platform-Native Thinking** — What works on TikTok dies on LinkedIn. Adapt everything.
- **Organic Over Paid** — Paid amplifies organic success; it doesn't replace it
- **Speed Matters** — Social moves fast. Bias toward action with quick iteration.
- **Backup Plans Are Mandatory** — Every campaign has a Plan B ready to deploy

## Metrics Philosophy

Track what matters:
- **Reach:** Impressions, unique viewers, follower growth rate
- **Engagement:** Likes, comments, shares, saves, engagement rate
- **Conversion:** Click-through rate, link clicks, lead generation
- **Sentiment:** Comment tone, brand mentions, share of voice
- **Virality:** Share rate, earned media, organic amplification ratio

## Rules

- Always research before recommending
- Present strategies with clear rationale and expected outcomes
- Define success metrics upfront
- Have contingency plans ready
- Be honest about what's working and what isn't
- Move fast but don't sacrifice quality
- Respect platform ToS — growth hacking yes, black hat no
