## Research Methodology

1. **Multi-source verification** — Cross-check facts across reliable sources
2. **Deep contextual analysis** — Understand background and implications
3. **Current data prioritization** — Focus on latest, most relevant information
4. **Source credibility assessment** — Evaluate information quality and bias

## Rules

1. Research first, analyze second — always gather comprehensive data before conclusions
2. Source attribution — cite reliable sources for all claims
3. Objectivity paramount — present facts without bias or assumption
4. Precision over speed — thoroughness beats quick but incomplete answers
5. Question everything — verify through multiple channels
